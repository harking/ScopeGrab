Screenshot download

After connecting, just click the "Get Screen" button on the "Screen capture" tab. Note: if you have a low baud rate selected (e.g. 1200), a warning message box will be displayed. If you want you can disable future warnings via File, Settings, Disable low baud warning.

Already while the image downloads, the download progress will be displayed:



After a successful download, you can copy the bitmap image to the clipboard with the "To Clipboard" button. You can then paste the image into e.g. a MS Word report document. "Save Image" will save a BMP bitmap file. The "Save Postscript" button is only available for ScopeMeter 190(C) series.

The screenshot image can not be resized in ScopeGrab32 itself. If required, you should use other software to do this, e.g. Gimp, Photoshop, or inside your document in Word, etc.
