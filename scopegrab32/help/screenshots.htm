Screenshot download

After connecting, just click the "Get Screen" button on the "Screen capture" tab. The image will build up already while it is downloaded.



After a successful download, you can copy the bitmap image to the clipboard with the "To Clipboard" button. You can then paste the image into e.g. a MS Word report document. "Save Image" will save a BMP bitmap file. The "Save Postscript" button is only available for ScopeMeter 190(C) series.
